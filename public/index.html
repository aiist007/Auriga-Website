05 FAQ
会员航班是怎么运作的？ How do member flights work? +

合作机队每周在美西执飞数百个私人航段。航单从中为会员精选——整舱、会员礼遇价、每周发布、先到先飞。细节由你的礼宾在数小时内确认。

Our fleet partners fly hundreds of private sectors weekly across the West. The List curates the finest of them for members — whole cabins at members' fare, released each week, first come, first flown. Your concierge confirms details within hours.

谁来执飞？ Who operates the aircraft? +

始终是 FAA Part 135 持证承运人。Auriga 是会员俱乐部与持牌包机经纪；每次预订前，执飞承运人都会向你完整披露。

FAA-certificated Part 135 air carriers, always. Auriga is a members' club and licensed charter broker; the operating carrier is disclosed to you before every booking.

可以带朋友和宠物吗？ Can I bring my people — and my dog? +

整舱是你的。带上你的人；宠物坐客舱，就在你身边。

The cabin is yours. Bring your five, and yes — dogs ride cabin, beside you.

会籍包含什么？ What does membership include? +

航单权限、俱乐部日历、会员引荐与专属礼宾。航班按次以会员礼遇价预订——会籍是钥匙，不是计价器。

Access to The List, the club calendar, member introductions, and your concierge. Flights are booked at members' fare, per trip — membership is the key, not the meter.

如何获得邀请？ How do I get invited? +

在下方提交申请，最好附上推荐会员。我们分小批审核——房间的质感，来自门槛。

Request below, ideally with a referring member. We review in small batches to keep the room what it is.

支付方式？ How can I pay? +

支持银行卡、电汇、USDC（持牌通道）；也支持支付宝/微信，账单可开具给家人。

Card, wire, or USDC via a licensed processor.
